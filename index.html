Hiya! My name is Edoardo and I am a 3rd-Year Ph.D. Candidate in Economics at Cornell University.

My research interest are political economy, media economics and development economics.

Prior to joining Cornell, I was a Research Assistant at the Stockholm School of Economics and at Queen Mary University of London. I also worked at the Organisation for Economic Co-Operation and Development, in the Development Co-Operation Directorate.

I hold a Master of Science in Economics with Distinction from the University of Warwick.
